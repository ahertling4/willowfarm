Frequently asked questions

What does a day at Green WIllows Farm look like?

The farm will be used from the hours of 9am to 3pm for day center classes. Like public school, the students will spend their days doing different classes together. Each student will also get one-on-one time to work on individual needs. 

(reading, writing, math, getting a job, etc.)

What is a typical student schedule?
  • 9-10am – Morning Circle- Reflect on what we will be doing that day. Talk about how we want to work on ourselves for that day. 
  • 10-11am – Meditation and Yoga
  • 11-Noon – Art class(Fridays)Lunch Prep
  • Noon-1 – Lunch – Gardening/ aquaponics
  • 1-2:00pm -Gardening/ aquaponics
  • 2-2:45pm -Whatever sport activity we have that day or week
  • 2:45-3pm – Down time. Gathering items to go back home
How will students get to the farm?

Parent can either drive them or we will have teachers pick up at various organizations. This is a great opportunity for partnerships and could be expanded over time.

When will students be able to use therapy?

During the day or at night. Different therapies will be seasonal based. Therapy will cost extra on top of day center classes. A student will not have to be enrolled in day classes to be able to use therapy resources. Depending on functionality level of student will be determined if allowed different therapies.
